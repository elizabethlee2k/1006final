Quarantine Activities to Try
  • Have a pool party! Get some friends on a zoom call, put on your swimsuit and get in a bathtub if you have one. Change your virtual background to a pool. Bring snacks that feel pool-party-ish to you. For me that means goldfish and watermelon.
  • Try doing an instructionless project. Bake cookies without a recipe and see how they turn out. Sew something without a pattern.
  • Make some small parachutes out of whatever you have around, and then parachute Barbies or some other toy out a window or off a porch. (Only appropriate for more rural dwellers)
  • Watch these newborn falcons live on a webcam
